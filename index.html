Biography

Hi, I’m Raqib! I’m a 3rd-year CS Ph.D. student at UNC Charlotte, advised by Prof. Dong Dai. I am affiliated with the DIR Lab, and Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory.

My research aims to build a high-performance graph processing engine to support trillion-edge graph processing in a single heterogeneous machine. In this regard, I want to exploit emerging storage technologies (e.g., Persistent Memory). I am particularly interested in developing data structures to efficiently store dynamic graphs without sacrificing the graph analysisâ€™s efficiency.
